BT Gym - iPhone Install

Do not open index.html directly from the iPhone Files app. iOS often blocks or partially loads local app files, which can show the interface without the workout data.

Best setup:

1. Upload this whole folder to a static website host such as Netlify, Vercel, Cloudflare Pages, GitHub Pages, or any normal web host.
2. Open the website link in Safari on your iPhone.
3. Tap Share.
4. Tap Add to Home Screen.
5. Open BT Gym from the new home-screen icon.

Will it save data?

Yes. Your loads, reps, completed exercises, notes, active week/day, and theme are saved on your iPhone in Safari storage. They remain there when you use the same home-screen app.

Do not clear Safari website data if you want to keep your logs.

Offline use:

After the first successful Safari visit from a real website link, the app caches itself for offline use. Open it once while online before taking it to the gym.
